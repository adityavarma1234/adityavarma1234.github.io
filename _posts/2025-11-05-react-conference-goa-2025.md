Just got back from the React Conference in Goa, held October 31 – November 2, 2025, and it was a fantastic experience. Beyond the sessions themselves, it turned out to be a really good opportunity to connect with fellow engineers, swap notes on what everyone's building, and put faces to a lot of names I'd only known from GitHub and Twitter.

A couple of tech talks stood out in particular — the sessions from the Atlassian and Jio Star teams were packed with practical, real-world insights, and I came back with a handful of ideas I'm already itching to try out.

Also, a big thank you to Nice Health Care for sponsoring the trip and making it possible for me to attend. Looking forward to the next one!
