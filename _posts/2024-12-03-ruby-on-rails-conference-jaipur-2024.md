Attended the Ruby on Rails Conference in Jaipur, held November 28–30, 2024, and came away with a bunch of useful takeaways.

A couple of insights stood out in particular — some great patterns around handling background workers cleanly, and practical approaches to handling things at scale once traffic and data start piling up.

As always, the best part was meeting fellow engineers and connecting with them — plenty of good conversations and a few new contacts I'm looking forward to staying in touch with.
