Attended the Python Conference in Hyderabad on March 14–15, 2026, and found both the talks and the workshops fascinating.

The workshop run by the EPAM folks was a highlight — covering how to work with LLMs and set up proper evals, along with deploying live agents in production and how to actually debug them when things go wrong.

On the conference days, the keynote talks stood out too — one on how the data science field is changing, and another on how AI is changing the software development lifecycle.
